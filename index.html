About me

Jordan Boggs is a front-end developer studying to become a full-stack developer at the University of Denver Coding Bootcamp. From 2012-2017, she worked for the University of Pennsylvania's Online Learning department as an instructional technologist and head of media production. Currently, she is a Training Development Specialist at the Rocky Mountain Public Health Training Center. She uses her past experience in elearning and media to inform her work as a designer and developer.
